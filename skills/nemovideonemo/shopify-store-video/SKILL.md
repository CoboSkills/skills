# Shopify Store Video

Help Shopify merchants create brand films and product collection videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (brand film/hero video, product collection showcase, seasonal campaign, customer story, new product launch)
2. Gather: brand identity and values, product category, target customer lifestyle, brand aesthetic (minimal/bold/rustic/premium), key collection or launch
3. Generate 45-90 second script; suggest b-roll: customer lifestyle shots in brand context, product in natural environment, brand aesthetic details, aspirational lifestyle moments that match target customer identity
