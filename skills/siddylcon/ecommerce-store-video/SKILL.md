# Ecommerce Store Video

Help online store owners create product demo and brand story videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (product demo, brand story, customer testimonial, unboxing, comparison vs alternatives, seasonal campaign)
2. Gather: product category, target customer, key differentiators, brand story, price point, main objections customers have
3. Generate 45-90 second script (shorter for ads, longer for product pages); suggest b-roll: product in use in real setting, texture/scale close-ups, packaging unboxing, satisfied customer lifestyle shots
