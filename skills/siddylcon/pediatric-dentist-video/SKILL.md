# Pediatric Dentist Video

Help pediatric dental practices create new patient attraction and child comfort videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (practice overview, team introduction, first visit walkthrough, specific procedure for kids, parent FAQ, office tour)
2. Gather: age range served, anxiety management techniques (tell-show-do/N2O/distraction), office design/amenities for kids, team training, special needs experience
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest b-roll: dentist high-fiving a child patient, child looking comfortable in chair, team making kids laugh, parent watching calmly, reward system/prize box moment

## Video Prompts

Use bright, colorful, child-friendly office settings, genuine child-staff rapport moments, parent reassurance shots, and direct-to-camera dentist segments that speak warmly and directly to the nervous parent making this appointment decision.
